BIOGRAPHY

My name is Xiaotian Guo (You can call me Sky in English). I am currently a Guest Researcher at University of Amsterdam. Thanks to my great Ph.D. supervisors, Prof. Andy Pimentel and Dr. Todor Stefanov, for supporting me in finishing this Ph.D.
Moreover, my research focuses on the distributed inference of deep learning models at the Edge. The principal goal of my Ph.D. project is to achieve scalable, efficient, robust, and secure DNN execution at the Edge.
My PhD thesis "Distributed DNN inference at the Edge" is available in the link. I have published 1 TCAD jouranl paper, 1 IoT journal paper, 1 ASP-DAC Conference paper, 1 ECML-PKDD workshop paper.
